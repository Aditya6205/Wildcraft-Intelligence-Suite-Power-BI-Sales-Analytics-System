Most businesses collect data. Few actually use it.
This project was built around a simple question: what if a dashboard could answer the 5 questions every business leader asks — before they even ask them?
The Wildcraft Intelligence Suite is a full-scale, end-to-end Power BI analytics system built on Wildcraft's sales data. It goes beyond tracking numbers — it surfaces the why behind performance, flags risks early, and recommends where to act next.
Total Revenue Analyzed: ₹259.79M

Sales Officers Tracked: 50

Cities Mapped: 53 white-space expansion opportunities identified

Pages Built: 6 fully interactive dashboards

📊 Dashboard Pages & Key Findings
Page 1 — Executive Revenue Overview
Business Question: Did we hit target, and what drove performance?

Total Revenue: ₹259.79M vs. Target: ₹258.06M → 100.67% achievement
Peak Month: June at ₹24.27M — 30.48% above November (weakest month at ₹18.60M)
Seasonal demand curve identified across 12 months
Gap between best and worst month: ₹5.67M — an inventory planning blind spot

Insight: Revenue achievement masked inconsistent monthly performance. The business was hitting annual targets but losing potential revenue in low months due to inventory misalignment with seasonal demand.

Page 2 — Sales Officer Performance
Business Question: Is our sales team structured correctly?

50 Sales Officers tracked individually
Top Performer: Ravi Pillai — ₹12.0M closed
Team Average: ₹5.20M per rep
Ravi outperformed the average by 2.3×
Bottom 20% of reps: under 1.5% of individual target achievement
Performance spread revealed disproportionate revenue concentration in top 5 reps

Insight: This wasn't a motivation problem — it was a structural one. Territory allocation, coaching gaps, and onboarding quality were creating a performance cliff between top and bottom performers.

Page 4 — Regional Risk & Geo-Spatial Analysis
Business Question: Which regions need intervention now?

Bengaluru: 3.5% of total national revenue — top city contributor
Udupi: 0.54% revenue share → flagged as #1 high-risk region (23% risk score)
Shivamogga & Davanagere: 19% risk score each
Geo-spatial heatmap built across all active cities
Risk scores calculated using forecasting variance + revenue trend deviation

Insight: Regional underperformance was invisible in standard sales reports. Geo-spatial visualization + risk scoring gave leadership an early warning system — not a post-mortem.

Page 5 — Forecasting & Trend Intelligence
Business Question: What should we expect next quarter?

DAX time-intelligence measures: YoY, MTD, QTD, Rolling 3-month
Forecast model built using Power BI's native AI forecasting
Seasonal adjustment applied based on 12-month historical trend
Confidence interval bands displayed for executive decision-making
Udupi flagged proactively using forecast deviation threshold

Insight: Moving from reactive reporting to proactive planning. Leadership can now see a likely trajectory — not just where they've been.

Page 6 — White-Space Expansion Analysis
Business Question: Where should the business grow next?

53 cities identified as high-opportunity, low-penetration markets
Ranking model built on: revenue potential index vs. current outlet density
Cities segmented into Tier 1 / Tier 2 / Tier 3 opportunity buckets
Output: a ranked, capital-allocation-ready expansion roadmap

Insight: Replaced executive intuition with a model-generated priority list. Every city on the list has a data rationale — not just a regional manager's preference.
